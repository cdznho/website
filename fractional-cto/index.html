Frequently asked questions

What does a Fractional CTO actually do?

Cedric acts as your embedded technical lead — setting AI and engineering strategy, reviewing architecture, hiring and managing engineers, running technical due diligence, and representing tech to investors, design partners, and the board.

How much does a Fractional CTO cost?

Engagements start at $12,000 per month for a 2-days-per-week Starter tier, over a 3–6 month minimum term. Larger scopes run to $18,000 / month (Core) or higher for Interim CTO coverage. Equity is possible for the right fit.

Who is this for?

Pre-seed to Series A AI-first startups without a technical co-founder or CTO, non-technical founders who need an engineering leader, teams pivoting into AI, and VCs needing technical diligence on portfolio companies.

What's the minimum commitment?

3 months minimum — shorter engagements don't have time to create real leverage. Most engagements run 6 months, at which point you either extend, move to advisory, or hand off to a full-time CTO.

Can you take equity instead of cash?

Partial equity is available for the right fit — typically a blended cash + equity package, not equity-only. This is discussed on the intro call once scope is clear.

What if I just need a one-off review or second opinion?

Start with a consulting session. That's the right shape of help for a one-off decision or an architecture review, without a 3-month commitment.

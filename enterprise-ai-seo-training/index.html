Frequently asked questions

What formats does enterprise AI SEO training come in?

Training is available as multi-seat team access to the online course, private cohort workshops (online or in-person), and structured multi-session cohort programmes for global teams.

How many people can join enterprise AI SEO training?

Team training starts at 5 participants. Private workshops and cohort programmes can accommodate teams of any size, including global rollouts across multiple offices and geographies.

Can the training be customised for our industry?

Yes. Custom programmes include industry-specific examples, case studies, and frameworks tailored to your sector, content architecture, and existing SEO maturity.

How long does a private workshop take?

Workshops are typically half a day (3–4 hours) or a full day (7 hours). Multi-session cohort programmes run over 4–8 weeks. All formats are flexible and confirmed when you enquire.

What if our team is new to AI SEO?

The programme is designed to bring intermediate-to-expert practitioners up to speed quickly. For teams with no SEO baseline, a foundational session can be added. Cedric will recommend the right starting point when you enquire.

AI SEO Course — Full Syllabus

5 modules · ~2 hours · Intermediate level · Enterprise-focused

The AI SEO Course for Enterprise Teams is structured across five modules. Each module builds on the previous, moving from foundational understanding to advanced implementation and measurement. Below is the complete lesson-by-lesson breakdown.

Module 1 — The AI Search Landscape

~25 minutes

Understand how AI search tools work and why enterprise brands that ignore this shift are losing visibility, trust, and pipeline.

  • How ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews generate answers
  • Why traditional SEO is no longer sufficient for enterprise visibility
  • The data: zero-click searches, AI traffic displacement, and citation economics
  • The enterprise visibility risk: what happens when your brand is invisible to AI
  • Worksheet: AI search audit starter Downloadable

Module 2 — AI SEO Strategy Framework

~30 minutes

Build a structured AI SEO strategy for your enterprise — from query mapping to priority scoring and team alignment.

  • The 5 pillars of AI SEO for enterprise organisations
  • Mapping your content to AI query categories and commercial intent
  • Identifying high-value AI citation opportunities in your sector
  • Competitive gap analysis: how to find what competitors are being cited for
  • Prioritising improvements across large-scale content archives
  • Template: AI SEO strategy planner Downloadable

Module 3 — Content Structure for AI Citations

~30 minutes

Learn the specific content formats, structures, and writing patterns that make AI models more likely to cite your brand in generated answers.

  • How AI models evaluate trustworthiness and citation eligibility
  • Direct-answer format: writing the content AI can extract and use
  • FAQ architecture and PAA (People Also Ask) optimisation
  • Comparison tables, definition blocks, and structured narrative formats
  • Schema markup essentials: Article, FAQPage, Course, Person, BreadcrumbList
  • Author attribution and E-E-A-T signals for enterprise content
  • Template: AI-optimised content brief Downloadable

Module 4 — Technical AI SEO & Crawl Strategy

~20 minutes

Ensure AI systems can find, access, and trust your content — covering crawlability, internal linking, and the technical signals that underpin AI citation authority.

  • robots.txt for AI crawlers: GPTBot, ClaudeBot, Google-Extended, Applebot-Extended
  • Sitemap strategy for enterprise AI visibility
  • Internal linking architecture for authority clustering
  • Canonicalisation and duplicate content impact on AI trust signals
  • Page speed and Core Web Vitals as AI readiness signals

Module 5 — Measurement & Competitive Intelligence

~15 minutes

Build a reporting framework for AI search visibility that satisfies both practitioners and senior stakeholders.

  • Tracking AI citation frequency across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini
  • Brand mention monitoring and AI share of voice
  • Monitoring competitive AI visibility in your category
  • Reporting AI SEO performance to boards and senior leadership
  • Template: AI SEO reporting dashboard Downloadable

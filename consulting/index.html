Process

The work starts with the bottleneck, not the tool.

1

Diagnose

Clarify the business bottleneck, current workflow, and decision that matters.

2

Prioritize

Choose the system that can create the most useful leverage now.

3

Design the system

Map the workflow, assets, data, prompts, tools, owners, and handoffs.

4

Implement or hand off

Build the first version or document it clearly enough for your team to own.

5

Measure

Decide what signal tells you whether the system should be improved, repeated, or retired.

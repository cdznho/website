Frequently asked questions

What happens on a consulting call?

You bring a specific problem — a plan you want pressure-tested, a decision you are stuck on, or a piece of work you want reviewed. Cedric joins prepared, works through it with you live, and you leave with a concrete next step.

How much does consulting cost?

Single sessions start from $500 per hour. Multi-session packages start at $2,500 for 5 hours. Monthly retainers start at $4,000 per month. Final pricing depends on scope.

What topics can I book a session on?

AI SEO and AI search visibility, content strategy for LLMs, applied AI for marketing teams, building AI-assisted workflows, technical implementation reviews, and go-to-market narrative for AI-native products.

Can you sign an NDA?

Yes. A mutual NDA is available on request before you share sensitive material. Enterprise clients can use their own standard NDA.

What if I need more than a one-off session?

Move to a multi-session package or monthly retainer. If you need embedded, ongoing leadership, look at Fractional CTO. If you are training a team, look at AI training.

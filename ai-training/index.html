Frequently asked questions

How is this different from the AI SEO course?

The AI SEO course is a focused self-paced programme on AI search visibility. AI training for companies is a custom, role-based workshop or programme on applied AI for day-to-day work — content, sales, ops, leadership — not just search.

How much does a custom AI training programme cost?

Half-day workshops start from $10,000. Full-day programmes start from $18,000. Multi-session rollouts for larger organisations are scoped per engagement.

Can training be customised by role?

Yes. Programmes are built around the specific workflows of your marketing, sales, operations, or leadership teams — with examples and exercises drawn directly from your own day-to-day work.

Can we run this in-person?

Yes. Online or in-person, EU or remote. Travel costs are billed at actuals on top of the programme fee.

How do you protect our confidential information?

A mutual NDA is available on request. Training material built on your examples is not reused for other clients.

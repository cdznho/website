Frequently asked questions

What is AI SEO?

AI SEO — also known as Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO) — is the practice of making your brand visible in AI-powered search tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews. Where traditional SEO targets Google rankings, AI SEO targets citations in AI-generated answers. Read the full AI SEO guide →

Who is the AI SEO course for?

The course is built for SEO managers, content leads, digital marketing teams, and heads of growth at enterprise organisations. It's especially valuable for teams already running traditional SEO who need to adapt to AI-generated search.

Is there a team licence option?

Yes. For teams of 5 or more, private workshops, and cohort training, visit the enterprise AI SEO training page or contact Cedric directly to discuss options.

How long does the course take?

The core course is approximately 2 hours of video content. All materials include downloadable resources and templates you can apply immediately. Access is lifetime.

Is AI going to replace SEO?

AI is not replacing SEO — it's changing what SEO means. The skills, signals, and structures that earn AI citations overlap significantly with good SEO fundamentals, but the measurement and strategy layers are different. Teams that adapt early hold a significant competitive advantage.

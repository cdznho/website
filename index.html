Frequently asked questions

What do you actually do?

Four main things: 1:1 consulting for specific decisions, Fractional CTO for embedded technical leadership at AI-first startups, custom AI training for companies, and self-paced online courses. See Work with me for the full picture.

How do I pick the right format?

A single decision or review — book a consulting session. Ongoing technical leadership for a startup — Fractional CTO. Training a team on AI — AI training. Learning at your own pace — the AI SEO course.

What is AI SEO?

AI SEO — also known as Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO) — is the practice of making your brand visible in AI-powered search tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews. Where traditional SEO targets Google rankings, AI SEO targets citations in AI-generated answers. Read the full AI SEO guide →

Do you work with individuals or just companies?

Both. Individual practitioners take the course or book a single consulting session. Companies engage me for training, Fractional CTO, or multi-session work.

Is AI going to replace SEO?

AI is not replacing SEO — it's changing what SEO means. The skills that earn AI citations overlap significantly with good SEO fundamentals, but the measurement and strategy layers are different. Teams that adapt early hold a compounding advantage.

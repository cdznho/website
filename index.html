About Cedric

I'm Cedric, based in the UK. By day I'm Head of Data & AI at Firgun Ventures, where I'm building an intelligence system to support investment decisions in the emerging Quantum Computing landscape. In parallel, I'm Lead AI Lecturer at Le Wagon — the #1-rated coding bootcamp worldwide — where I've taught Data Science, Data Engineering, Data Analytics, and GenAI for 4+ years.

For Le Wagon B2B, I've designed and delivered AI programmes for teams at Warner Music, SLB, ODDO, Engie, Stellantis, Eiffage, Roland Berger, and Bain & Company — and authored their GenAI for Data Scientists bootcamp, Advanced RAG course, and GenAI & SQL course. I also run Agentic SEO, a boutique AI SEO consultancy, and I'm the author of an Amazon bestselling book on AI.

Before Firgun I was Technical Co-Founder of an e-commerce brand, a Data Engineer / PowerBI Consultant at element61 (Belgium's most experienced BI & Data Science firm), a Technology Consultant at Deloitte, and Acting Chief of Staff at Culture Trip (20M monthly visitors), reporting directly to the CEO. What is AI SEO?

AI SEO — also known as Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO) — is the practice of making your brand visible in AI-powered search tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews. Where traditional SEO targets Google rankings, AI SEO targets citations in AI-generated answers. Read the full AI SEO guide →

Do you work with individuals or just companies?

Both. Individual practitioners take the course or book a single consulting session. Companies engage me for training, a Fractional Head of Data & AI role, or multi-session work.

Is AI going to replace SEO?

AI is not replacing SEO — it's changing what SEO means. The skills that earn AI citations overlap significantly with good SEO fundamentals, but the measurement and strategy layers are different. Teams that adapt early hold a compounding advantage.

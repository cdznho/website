AI Growth SystemsVisual Identity — 16 / 24
Typography — Secondary typefaces

Archivo & IBM Plex Mono

Archivo — body & UI

Set in 400–600 weight for paragraphs, buttons, navigation, and card copy. It's the workhorse: legible at small sizes, neutral enough to let the display face and the accent colors do the talking.

IBM Plex Mono — labels

Uppercase, wide-tracked, used only for kickers, tags, data labels, and footer metadata — never for body copy. It signals "this is a system" the way the loop diagram does visually.

Brand treatment — green duotone

AI Growth SystemsVisual Identity — 21 / 24
Graphic style — Shapes, patterns & textures

Dot grids & the loop motif

A faint dot grid (the hero background), concentric rings around the logomark for emphasis states, and a fine line grid on dark surfaces. Used sparingly — texture supports content, it doesn't compete with it.

AI Growth SystemsVisual Identity — 22 / 24
Graphic style — Layout principles

Five rules

1. Grid & structure

1240px max content width, 28px side padding, 2-column grids for paired content.

2. Density & space

Generous vertical rhythm between sections; never crowd more than one idea per screen.

3. Hierarchy & focus

One kicker, one headline, one lead paragraph, then evidence — in that order, every time.

4. The loop as anchor

Where a page needs a centerpiece graphic, default to the logomark or pillar icons before inventing something new.

5. Paper first

Dark surfaces are reserved for section breaks and emphasis — most of the brand lives on paper.

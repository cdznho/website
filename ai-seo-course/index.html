Why AI SEO is different for enterprise teams

Enterprise organisations face unique AI SEO challenges that smaller sites do not. Large content archives, multiple sub-domains, inconsistent schema implementation, and decentralised content teams all compound the visibility problem.

This course addresses the enterprise-specific layers: how to audit at scale, how to prioritise high-value query categories for AI citations, how to align technical SEO and content teams around AI search signals, and how to measure AI visibility performance.

  • Covers AI-specific trust signals and authority building
  • Includes large-scale content audit methodology
  • Addresses governance for multi-team environments
  • Goes beyond basics to measurable outcomes

Frequently asked questions

What is included in the AI SEO course?

The course includes 2 hours of structured video instruction, downloadable templates and frameworks, module quizzes, lifetime email support, a certificate of completion, and lifetime access including future updates.

Is there a team licence option?

Yes. For teams of 5 or more, private cohort workshops, and custom enterprise training programmes, visit the enterprise AI SEO training page or contact Cedric directly.

What level is the course?

The course is pitched at intermediate level. It assumes basic familiarity with SEO fundamentals and is designed for practitioners in enterprise or agency environments, not beginners.

What if I already do traditional SEO?

This course is built specifically for experienced SEO professionals adapting to AI-generated search. It builds on traditional SEO knowledge and explains where the strategies diverge and why.

Can AI replace SEO?

AI is not replacing SEO — it is changing what SEO means. Winning AI citations requires many of the same fundamentals as traditional SEO (authority, structure, trust) plus AI-specific layers. This course covers both.
